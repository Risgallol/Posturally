Privacy

This demo processes your camera stream entirely on your device inside your web browser.

  • No video frames are uploaded or stored by us.
  • The posture model file is downloaded once from a public CDN, then used locally.
  • You can click “Stop camera” at any time to immediately disable the camera stream.
  • Open your browser’s developer tools → Network tab to verify no uploads occur.

If you fork and modify this project, you are responsible for any additional analytics or network requests you add.
